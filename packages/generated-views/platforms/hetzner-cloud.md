## Documented friction gates

- Hetzner advises avoiding VPNs and free email addresses and warns that incomplete or false details can delay or reject registration. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S2.
- A non-universal fraud check can require ID submission or credit-card prepayment, with no published review time or universal trigger. Stage 4. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2.
- Payment methods and automation vary by country and currency, while billing is retroactive and invoices are due immediately with a ten-day payment window before reminders. Stage 6. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- An invitation-created light account can enter another project but cannot create its own projects or products until it supplies full customer and payment information. Stage 7. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S10.
- Shared and dedicated server families expose price, CPU isolation, account-limit, and first-order review tradeoffs. Stage 10. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2, S5, S6.
- Server price excludes Primary IPs. The user must choose IPv4, IPv6, both, or a private Network, each with different cost and reachability. Stage 13. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S5, S6, S8.
- The public key must be selected at server creation because Hetzner Console cannot add one afterward. Stage 14. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S5, S9.
- A Firewall restricts traffic, and its inbound rules drop connections not explicitly allowed. A first-path Firewall therefore needs a rule that permits the intended SSH connection. Stage 15. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S5, S14.
- Limits, unpaid invoices, deactivation, capacity, location restrictions, or a large or implausible first order can stop or delay CREATE & BUY NOW. Stage 17. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2, S10.
- For a lost SSH key, Hetzner documents Rescue boot, chroot, editing `/root/.ssh/authorized_keys`, and reboot. VNC is available for emergencies after a root-password reset when necessary. Stage 19. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S9, S12.

## Unknowns

- Which exact fields and agreement controls appear in the current direct registration form? Why unresolved: The account guide says to enter all required information but does not list every form field or checkbox. Next evidence needed: The number of registration inputs and clicks cannot be enumerated beyond identity, contact, billing, currency, optional phone, and email confirmation.
- Which accounts or first orders trigger ID or credit-card prepayment verification, and how long does review take? Why unresolved: The fraud FAQ confirms possible verification and manual review but intentionally does not publish its risk rules, amounts, or timing. Next evidence needed: This can become the largest pre-server delay or cause rejection.
- Is adding a stored payment method mandatory before every first Cloud server, or can some regions order under a manual retroactive invoice method? Why unresolved: The account guide lists payment addition as a common next step, while the payment guide supports both stored automatic and manual invoice methods and does not state one universal pre-order rule. Next evidence needed: One billing page may or may not occur before CREATE & BUY NOW.
- Which plan, location, and Linux image should be treated as the universal first-server default? Why unresolved: The creation guide requires the developer to choose and provides category advice without naming one canonical combination. Next evidence needed: Exact price, architecture, capacity, availability, and operating-system behavior vary.
