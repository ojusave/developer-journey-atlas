## Documented friction gates

- A Twilio trial account is required for the common self-service route; no credit card is required to start it. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- The trial expires after 30 days and grants product-specific free units instead of a shared dollar balance, so the selected product determines the usable allowance. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- Signup requires email verification. Stage 3. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- Signup requires a reachable personal phone number and verification. Stage 4. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- Project/product answers customize the Console and can steer later onboarding. Stage 5. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- Twilio requires a product/channel choice before a meaningful first success exists. Stage 7. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2, S3.
- Trial product paths can have geography, verified-recipient, content, daily-message, phone-number, and regulatory restrictions. Stage 7. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3, S4.
