## Documented friction gates

- The email route depends on receiving and using a passwordless authorization code; no delivery time, expiry, resend, or invalid-code recovery is documented. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3.
- Free-cluster creation requires a preferred cloud provider and region; the Quickstart does not prescribe a universal best location. Stage 4. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1.
- Official guidance conflicts on whether payment details are required before the free cluster: the general guide requires them before cluster creation, while the Free Tier route omits them and is labeled free forever. Stage 4. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S4, S8, S9.
- Qdrant's product page says console deployment takes only a few seconds but publishes no timeout or recovery sequence. Stage 5. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S7.
- The initial database API key will not be shown again, so the documented prevention is to copy it and store it safely when prompted. Stage 6. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1.
- The current free and paid model list is exposed in the cluster's Inference tab; the selected model is explicitly identified as free in the Quickstart, and Qdrant states that free models are hosted in the US region only. Stage 10. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S6.
- If data growth exceeds 1 GB RAM or 4 GB disk, the documented recovery is to upgrade to Standard and scale the cluster. Stage 13. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S4.
