## Documented friction gates

- Google and GitHub are peer OAuth signup providers, and the choice changes the identity and consent route. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2.
- The project key is displayed only once and must be saved immediately. Stage 5. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S3.
- Platform access requires a supported card; bank or provider verification can add a confirmation step. Stage 8. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S6, S7.
- There is no free trial and at least $5 of prepaid credits is required before API calls. Stage 9. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S5.
- A credit purchase can remain pending for up to 15 minutes before the balance updates. Stage 9. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S7.
