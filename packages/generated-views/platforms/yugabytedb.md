## Documented friction gates

- The signup application's static unauthenticated response requires JavaScript, and official docs do not expose the form or account-creation sequence needed to reach login. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S3, S7, S8.
- Email-based, social, and federated authentication are named, but no complete signup candidate is documented. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S3, S8.
- In the documented downstream wizard, the database credential file must be saved; the official Sandbox guide states that losing it means the database cannot be used. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2.
- A downstream Sandbox pauses after 10 days inactive. The documented recovery is to sign in, select the cluster, and click Resume; if not resumed, it is deleted after 15 days. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S2, S5.
- A downstream Cloud Shell session lasts one hour and may disconnect after five idle minutes. The documented recovery is to close the browser tab and connect again. Stage 2. Evidence state: documented requirement. Sources: S1, S4.
